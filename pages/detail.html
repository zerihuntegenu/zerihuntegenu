English Translation

WHEREAS, it has been realized as is necessary to ensure rule of law through organizing the public prosecutor’s activities hitherto performed by various institutions in unsystematic way and delivering efficient, quality and accessible services;

WHEREAS, it is found necessary to establish an effective public prosecution institution that win the public trust and attain professional and institutional independence so as to provide transparent, accountable and participatory public prosecution services;

WHEREAS, it is found necessary to establish Attorney General Institution for the respect of the constitutional and legal system in the region and for ensuring rule of law and protection of basic rights and freedoms of citizens enshrined in the constitution so it is important to establish Attorney General Office Of Oromia Regional state .NOW, THEREFORE,proclamation No.214/2018 is proclaimed . Accordingly Daaroo Labuu attorney office, Starting from 2013 established result based planning. By doing so is the office is working on constructing justice system that make sure of the realization of over mentioned aims . The plan have 3 categories of goals set to be achieved : four general goals, four medial goals and 18 immediate goals

Mission

The Office of Attorney General shall have the following objectives:

  • Respecting and protecting the constitution and the constitutional order
  • Ensuring rule of law
  • Safeguarding the criminal law
  • Protecting the civil interest of the Regional State and the public.

Vission

By 2026 ,the office have vision to became number one in providing service and justice that is fair and speedy in daaroo labuu wereda.And olso to have public truest at large .

Core values

  • Rule of law
  • Protecting the civil interest of the Regional State and the public.
  • Independence of work
  • Responsibility
  • Transparency
  • Exemplary
  • Disgusting Bad practice and corruption
  • Inclusiveness
  • Quality service
  • Ready for change and accommodating
  • Good sense of customer service
  • Culture of working Collaboratively
Oromic Translation
Seensaa

Ergaa labsii Mana Hojii Abbaa Alangaa Waliigalaa Mootummaa Naannoo Oromiyaa Hundeessuuf Bahe Lak.214/2011, Hojii Abbaa Alangummaa qaamolee adda addaatiin bifa faffaca’een hojjatamaa ture walitti qindeessuun hojii abbaa alangummaa si’ataa, qulqulluufi dhaqqabamaa ta’e kennuu

Olaantummaa seeraa mirkaneessuun barbaachisaa ta’uun waan itti amanameef;Tajaajila ogummaa abbaa alangummaa iftoominaafi itti gaafatamummaa qabu hirmaachisaa ta’e kennuuf Mana hojii Abbaa Alangaa bu’aa qabeessa, amantaa ummataa qabu, akkasumas bilisummaa ogummaafi dhaabbatummaa gonfate hundeessuun barbaachisaa ta’ee waan argameef

Naannicha keessatti sirni heeraafi seeraa akka kabajamuufi ol’aantummaan seeraa akka mirkanaa’u, akkasumas, mirgootaafi bilisummaawwan bu’uuraa lammiilee heeraafi seeraan eegaman kabajamuu isaanii mirkaneessuuf Mana Hojii Abbaa Alangaa Waliigalaa hundeessuun barbaachisaa ta’ee waan argameef; labsiin 214/2011 labsamee jira . bu’uraa Kanaan manii hojiichaa gubaa hangaa jalaatti mana hojii irraa deebi’ee hundeessuun hojii tti galee jira.

Manii hojii Abbaa Alangaa Aanaa Daaroo Labuus ijaarsa sirna haqaa biyyaa keenyaa mirkaneessuu keessatti akka qaama abbaa alangaa tokkootti shooraa ol’aanaa fi tumsa dagaagina sirna haqaa taasisuuf karoora bu'aa irratti xiyyeeffate (Result Based Planning) baafachuun hojii keessa galee kan jiru dha.

Bara 2013 kana irraa eegalee manni hojii keenyaa Karoora Bu'aa Irratti xiyyeeffate (KBIX) qopheeffachuun hojii keessa galee raawwii gaarii galmeessisuutti jira. Kunis karoora tarsiimoo KGT 3ffaa (Karoora Waggaa 10) mana hojii qabatan bu’uura godhachuudhaan bu’aalee sadarkaan jiran (bu'aa waliigalaa, bu'aa jiddu galeessaa fi bu'aa battalee) gidduu galeessa godhachuun karoora kana akkaataa qabatamaa mana hojii keenyaatiin Mul’ataa fi Ergama mana hojii keenya bira gahuu qabnu giddu galeessa godhachuun karooraa baafachuun hojiitti galee kan jiru dha.

Haalumaa Kanaan bu'aa waliigalaa Uummanni tajaajila manii hojii abbaa alangaa kennu irratti amantaa horatameera kan jedhu arguuf manni hojii Abbaa Alanga Aanaa Daaroo Labuu bu'aa giddu galeessa 4 (afur) fi bu'aa battalaa 18 (Kudha saddeet) qabatee hojiitti galuun kenniinsa tajaajila keenyaa hawaasa bal'aa biratti haala bu'aa sadarkaan jiru galmeeessisuu danda'uun hojachhuuf karoorfatee jira.

Bifuma walfakkaatuun sochiiwwaan mana hojii jijjiirama akka naannoo keenyaa fi biyyaatti dhufaa jiru milkeessuuf qooda bahachaa turan fi jiraniin kenniinsa tajaajila abbaa alanguumaa sadarkaa barbaadamuun gahuu fi gaaffii tajaajila haqaa uummatni kaasaa turee fi kaasaa jiruuf deebii walmadaalaa kennuudhaaf Manni Hojii Abbaa Alangaa Aanaa Daaroo Labuus karooraa BIX baafatee bu’aa irratti xiyyeeffachuudhaan hojiitti galee jira.

Ergama

Aanaa Daaroo Labuu keessatti namoota heeraa fi seera darbanii fi gocha yakkaatiin shakkaman seeratti dhiyeessuun murtii saffisaa fi haqa qabeessa ta’e itti kennisiisuun ol’aantummaa seeraa mirkaneessisuudhaan nagaa fi tasgabbii uummatichaa eegsisuu, dantaa hariiroo hawaasaa ummataa fi mootuummaa kabachiisuu, ragaa seera qabeessa ta’e galmeessuu, hubannoo seeraa hawaasichaa gabbisuu, seerotni sadarkaa isaanii eegataniifi heera mootummaan walsiman akka wixineeffamanii fi bifa wal fakkaatuun hojii irra akka oolu taasisuu.

Mul’ata

Bara 2017 tti Aanaa Daaroo Labuu keessatti tajaajila Abbaa Alangummaa raawwii caalmaa qabu kennuun sirna haqaa amantaa uummataa horate uumuu dha.
